SSR22-23-064 Project
Objective

The main aim of the project is to utilize our free weekend time in a productive way to mental relaxation in the natures bed and encourage and inspire people around us to get evolved in the weekend farming.

About Our Nature

Nature is always self-sustainable to get the ecological balance. As the human body cures its diseases and gets to the normal state in the same way nature also cures itself from all the destructions and natural calamities and fixes itself. If we won’t cause a major destruction to it then it’s always balanced.

Our Fields

To utilize available space and nutrients to the maximum extent.
To safeguard against hazards of weather, diseases and pests.
To provide balanced cattle feed.
To avail distribution of labour throughout the year.
To get handy instalments of cash returns.

Our Contribution

We as a computer engineer would usually have an off on weekends so going with some relaxation and a relief from the mental stress can plan some nature vibes as a part of it we came up with our plan of “Weekend Farming”, In the present busy day’s it may not be possible to do farming throughout the day but can’t we question ourselves to do it at least in the weekends as a part of weekend farming. As a part of weekend farming and gardening we collaborated with the Ashram International people, we volunteered in gardening activities along with them. The main aim of the project is improving the various crop mixtures, along with the extensive animal habitat with modern techniques.

Phases of Our Project

Creating awareness among the students and how can they enjoy it rather than taking it as a work. Encouraging students at our college and locals to be a part of the gardening .

Phase-1

Collecting content and saving for the PPT.

Phase-2

For about 2 to 3 hrs doing work in the farm every Saturday and Sunday mornings.

Phase-3

Creating awareness among our friends and other college mates through various mediums.

Phase-4

Implementing the modern techniques, higher crop yield with minimal physical work.

Phase-5

Learn all about farming and gardening. Would love to teach our experienced knowledge with anyone.
